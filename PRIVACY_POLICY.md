# Privacy Policy

**Algo Canvas**
**Effective Date: March 25, 2026**

## Overview

Algo Canvas is a free, open-source algorithm visualization app. Your privacy is not just respected — it is guaranteed by design. The app has no capability to collect, transmit, or store any personal data.

## Data Collection

Algo Canvas collects **no data whatsoever**. Specifically:

- **No personal information** — no name, email, phone number, or account of any kind
- **No usage data** — no analytics, no telemetry, no event tracking
- **No crash reports** — no crash reporting services or error logging to external servers
- **No device information** — no device identifiers, hardware info, or OS details are collected
- **No location data** — no GPS, IP-based, or network-based location tracking
- **No cookies or tracking technologies** — none

## Network Access

Algo Canvas makes **zero network calls**. The app operates entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet for any reason — there are no remote servers, no APIs, no cloud services, and no remote assets.

## Third-Party Services

Algo Canvas uses **no third-party services** that collect data. There are no ad networks, no analytics providers, no social media SDKs, and no embedded third-party content.

## Data Storage

The only data stored on your device is your theme preference (light/dark mode and accent color), saved locally using the platform's standard preferences mechanism. This data never leaves your device.

## Children's Privacy

Algo Canvas does not collect any data from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in the source repository and the effective date above will be updated. Because the app collects no data, material changes to this policy are unlikely.
